Meet Grant

A curious soul in a world with endless mysteries. I delight in disecting what most people take for granted so I can understand it and build it back together in new and interesting ways.

I enjoy understanding the mechanisms that weave our ever more intricate social fabric. I believe that technology is as much a tool for production and change as it is for education and enrichment.

I want to make things that connect people to the world they live in but do not know.
